Crab Chips

A cute blue crab shape snack may be put on an index finger for both joy and taste, and it is enjoyable anywhere and anytime for everyone. The snack contains about 6.5% of blue crab from Korea. Moreover, it is not fried in oil but baked with heated salt for more crunchy and clean taste. The unique taste and shape of Crab Chips are recognized even outside of Korea, and especially in Russia, it became the representative snack of Korea. It is now becoming the snack of the world.
