White Rabbit Candy

One of the most ubiquitous candies in China, almost every Chinese kid has munched on them at some point. Chinese grandparents often have a supply specifically to give to their grandchildren. Don’t try to take off the rice-wrapping layer around the main body of the candy because it’s supposed to be eaten with the candy and completely edible! It may be tough to chew at first, but if you keep chewing at this small cylindrically shaped candy your mouth will quickly be filled with the taste of vanilla.
