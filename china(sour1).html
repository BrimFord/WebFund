Li Hing Mui Drops

A hard candy outside with a Li Hing Mui (Dried Salted Plum) center. Like a lollipop, but without the bothersome stick. If the candy is a little sticky, pop it into the freezer. The candy will harden and you should be able to pop it right out of the wrapper.
